WHAT AMUSES ME

by Ashley Wasilchin

It can be easy to make me laugh, but it has to be the right thing. Some people will find something funny and I will find it dumb or not see why exactly it is funny.

To get to know me better, here are three different things that I find funny.

Animals and Pets

I love animals of all kinds. I also love animals when they are being dumb or when funny things happen to them. I could watch these videos for hours. Here is a video of tons of animals being dumb and doing dumb things.

Animals being dumb

Funny Kids

Not only do I like animals being funny and dumb, I also laugh at kids saying dumb and funny stuff. I especially think it's funny when they say things that they don't realize are wrong or dumb.

Kids saying funny things

Dumb Adults

The final thing I could watch for hours is adults having the mind of a five year old and doing something dumb that they think won't hurt them or when they think that nothing could go wrong.

Adults Making Dumb Decisions
